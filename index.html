About

Hello there, I'm Sam, I'm a passionate pixel artist. For the last three years I have been doing my bachelor's in computer applications but that's when I realised my love for pixel art. Through my hard work and dedication I hope to become better at creating pixel art. My main focus is in creating gaming environments and character assets.

How did I realise my passion for pixel art?

By playing games, yes! I used to play indie games in my free time and that's how I came to know that these beautiful art pieces were made from tiny squares. It intrigued me so I researched about it and wanted to learn. So ye that's the story of me embarking on a journey to learn pixel art :D. From then on I have been creating pixel art. Day by day I was able to see the progress. I'm self taught but still my love for pixel art keeps me going every single day.
